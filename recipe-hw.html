My broke student recipes




What are we eating?

Today you are going to learn how to make the most basic of the basic and maybe mildly depressing mac and cheese. I'm sure I once took this from a really good recipe, but so much has been forgotten that we now are stuck with this basic mess.

Perfect for that broke college student who has no will to go on but just enough will to not find Craft Mac and Cheese as an acceptable dinner option.





Step 1:Ingredients

In order to get started we need to know what we need! The next time you are at the store make sure you have all of these items.

  • 8 oz box of elbow macaroni(or any bite sized pasta)
  • Cheese of your choice
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 1/4 cup and 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 1/2-3 tablespoons of flour


Step 2-Complicated and annoyting but delicious

Cook the noodles as directed, with the replacement of milk instead of water.

Be careful!

Milk is a brat to cook with sometimes. Do not let it bubble and boil as you would with normal water. Keep a close eye on it and your meal will treat you a lot better.



Step 3

As or after you cook the noodles, melt butter in a smaller suacepan. The flour is going to be used to thicken it just a little bit so add in as you deem necessary. Some find it weird so it's fully your choice but it's the bunk way to do things.



Step 4

Stir cheese aggressively and thoroughly and now serve! Don't wait for it to cool. If you don't burn your tongue then you're not embracing the broke student experience.
