CareerWise

Earn while you learn in highschool with an apprenticeship!

Take Me There!
CareerWise is a non-profit here in Colorado giving highschool students the opportunity to enter the workforce in an important trade or service where they get the chance to earn as they learn the position. Every year they have a large variety of companies and positions to offer to the next talented students between 10th and 12th grade at offered schols. For the next 3 to 4 years the student is mentored outside of school to continue their progress and lead them on the road to success. After that amount of time is the student is still with the company they might even be offered to continue their position.
